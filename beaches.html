South Beach

There are many beaches on the Island. Going to the beach is the main thing to do on the Island. South Beach is located in Edgartown. It is a sandy beach with little rocks. There is plenty of parking along the street. There can be very big waves there sometimes but there are lifeguards there.

Another beach is Menemsha Beach. Menemsha Beach is located in the town of Menemsha. It is a rocky beach, with great sunsets because it is on the western side of the Island. It is on the outskirts of the Island and it can take a while to get to.

Moshup Beach is another beach on the Island. It is located in Aquinnah. It has very pretty cliffs. There are some big rocks on the beach and parking is limited. I recommend going to at least one of these beaches.
