Martha's Vineyard is a popular place for tourists to come and visit. You may know Martha's Vineyard from the movie Jaws which was filmed on the Island but Martha's Vineyard is also known for its beautiful beaches, restaurants and historical landmarks. There are only two ways to come to the Island, by boat or air. Several Presidents including President Obama and President Clinton have vacationed on the Island.

Martha's Vineyard is an Island seven miles off of Massachusetts and is next to Nantucket. It is twenty miles long and has an area of eighty seven miles squared. Martha's Vineyard is divided into six towns, with 15,000 residents all together. Martha's Vineyard used to be only inhabited by the Wampanoag tribe but later it became inhabited by the English. Martha's Vineyard got its start in the whaling industry and later became part of the tourist industry. Martha's Vineyard is a great summer time vacation destination.
